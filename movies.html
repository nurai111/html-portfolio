My Favorite Movies

Parasite

Parasite is a compelling social satire with an intriguing and captivating plot that masterfully examines class conflict through surprising turns. Viewers are kept on edge by its deft transition between dark comedy and gripping drama.

Fallen Angels

Fallen Angels enthralls with its evocative, dark cinematography and a profoundly personal and beautiful storyline. The film's gloomy, neon-lit ambiance aptly captures urban loneliness and ephemeral interpersonal bonds.

Parasite in Love

With a distinctive narrative that unconventionally examines obsession and mental difficulties, Parasite in Love offers an idiosyncratic and thought-provoking relationship. The beautiful yet eerie ambiance of the movie heightens the characters' emotional complexity.
